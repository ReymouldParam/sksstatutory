About SKS Statutory services

Simplifying Compliance Since

SKS Statutory services was born from a simple observation: Indian businesses, especially SMEs, were spending enormous energy navigating the maze of labour law compliance — energy that should go into growing their business.

Our founders — experienced HR and legal professionals — built LEAP to be the compliance backbone for businesses across India. Based in Mumbai, we serve clients from startups to large enterprises, across industries and geographies.

Today, LEAP manages compliance for 200+ clients across 15+ states, with a 100% on-time filing record and zero penalty instances for clients under our management.
